NYU Go

Inspired by Amazon Go, NYU Go is an app designed to make easy and quick purchases with the click of a camera.

NYU Go initially requires you log in and once you have, it takes you direct access to submit an image or take one of a barcode. Once you have submitted an image, it reads the image and price and asks for you to confirm your purchase. When confirming, the user is taken to a page filled with his/her previous transactions.

Rendezvous

Coming up plans with friends has always been a major pain. And that's how Rendezvous can come to play!

By entering the city you are at, Rendezvous will come up with an itinery for the day based on the weather and places nearby to eat. My team and I used a geolocation API to obtain the user's location, Yahoo's weather API to obtain the temperature, and Foursquare's API to come up with the places to hang out or eat at.

Foodifai

Foodifai allows users to submit a photo either from their library or by taking a new photo. Users never have to enter in a single line of text.
